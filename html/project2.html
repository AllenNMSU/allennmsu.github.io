A Family Trip Through the Philippines, Singapore, and Vietnam

This website describes some of the amazing places our family visited during a trip through Southeast Asia. Each country had unique culture, landmarks, and natural beauty that made the experience unforgettable.

From the islands of the Philippines to the modern city of Singapore and the scenic landscapes of Vietnam, every destination offered something special. Below are some of the places we explored during the journey.

Places We Visited

  • Philippines: Cebu and Bohol
  • Singapore: ArtScience Museum, Gardens by the Bay Flower Dome, and the Marina Bay Sands light show
  • Vietnam: Da Nang, the Golden Bridge, and Ba Na Hills (often called the "Vietnam Disneyland")
